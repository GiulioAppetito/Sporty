FR

1. The system shall provide a keywords-based search. 

2. The system shall provide an upload images feature.

3. The system shall provide a profile customization page.



Dictionary

Keywords: sports, places, users..

Profile customization: for a simple user: name, surname, email address, age, profile picture, biography;
		       for an organization (such as a sport center, gym, swimming pool): name, photos, bio (address, phone number), time tables, courses;
